      <p><b>Moldova</b> is a country in <a href="/wiki/Eastern_Europe" title="Eastern Europe">Eastern Europe</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">[14]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">[15]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16">[16]</a></sup> Its full name is the <b>Republic of Moldova</b> (<a href="/wiki/Romanian_language" title="Romanian language">Romanian</a>: <i lang="ro">Republica Moldova</i>, <span class="unicode haudio"><span class="fn"><span style="white-space:nowrap;margin-right:.25em;"><a href="/wiki/File:Ro-Moldova.ogg" title="About this sound"><img alt="About this sound" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/8a/Loudspeaker.svg/11px-Loudspeaker.svg.png" decoding="async" width="11" height="11" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/8a/Loudspeaker.svg/17px-Loudspeaker.svg.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/8a/Loudspeaker.svg/22px-Loudspeaker.svg.png 2x" data-file-width="20" data-file-height="20"></a></span><a href="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/9/90/Ro-Moldova.ogg" class="internal" title="Ro-Moldova.ogg">listen</a></span>&nbsp;<small class="metadata audiolinkinfo" style="cursor:help;">(<a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Media_help" title="Wikipedia:Media help"><span style="cursor:help;">help</span></a>·<a href="/wiki/File:Ro-Moldova.ogg" title="File:Ro-Moldova.ogg"><span style="cursor:help;">info</span></a>)</small></span>.) It used to be called <b>Moldavia.</b> </p>
      <p>The official language in Moldova is the <a href="/wiki/Romanian_language" title="Romanian language">Romanian language</a>. The <a href="/wiki/Capital_(city)" class="mw-redirect" title="Capital (city)">capital</a> of Moldova is <a href="/wiki/Chi%C8%99in%C4%83u" title="Chișinău">Chișinău</a>. </p>
      <p>The <a href="/wiki/Russian_Empire" title="Russian Empire">Russian Empire</a> took almost half of the <a href="/wiki/Moldavia" title="Moldavia">Principality of Moldova</a> in 1812 and called it <a href="/wiki/Bessarabia" title="Bessarabia">Bessarabia</a>. Between 1918 - 1940 and 1941-1944 it was part of the <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Romania" title="Kingdom of Romania">Kingdom of Romania</a>. It became independent from the <a href="/wiki/Soviet_Union" title="Soviet Union">Soviet Union</a> after its dissolution. </p>
      <p>The average monthly salary/wages are still low, standing at only $243 USD.<sup id="cite_ref-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-17">[17]</a></sup> Moldova is an associated country for future membership in the <a href="/wiki/European_Union" title="European Union">European Union</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">[18]</a></sup> </p>
