      <p><b>Honduras</b> is a country in <a href="/wiki/Central_America" title="Central America">Central America</a>. Its capital is <a href="/wiki/Tegucigalpa" title="Tegucigalpa">Tegucigalpa</a>. Most people of Honduras speak the Spanish language. 7,483,763 people live in Honduras and it is 112,492 square kilometres (43,433&nbsp;sq&nbsp;mi) in size. It is next to <a href="/wiki/El_Salvador" title="El Salvador">El Salvador</a>. To one side is the <a href="/wiki/Caribbean_Sea" title="Caribbean Sea">Caribbean Sea</a> and to the other in the <a href="/wiki/Pacific_Ocean" title="Pacific Ocean">Pacific Ocean</a>. </p>
      <p>The ethnic makeup of Honduras is 90% mestizo, 7% amerindian, 2% black, and 1% white.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> </p>
      <p>Honduras has the world's highest <a href="/wiki/Murder" title="Murder">murder</a> rate. </p>
