      <p><b>Mauritania</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Country" title="Country">country</a> in northwest <a href="/wiki/Africa" title="Africa">Africa</a>. The capital city, which is also the biggest city in the country, is <a href="/wiki/Nouakchott" title="Nouakchott">Nouakchott</a>. It is on the <a href="/wiki/Atlantic_Ocean" title="Atlantic Ocean">Atlantic</a> coast. Its president is General Mohamed Oueld Abdel-Aziz. </p>
      <p>At 1,030,700 <a href="/wiki/Square_kilometre" title="Square kilometre">km²</a> (397,929 <a href="/wiki/Square_miles" class="mw-redirect" title="Square miles">mi²</a>),<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> Mauritania is the world's 29th-largest country (after <a href="/wiki/Bolivia" title="Bolivia">Bolivia</a>). It is similar in size to <a href="/wiki/Egypt" title="Egypt">Egypt</a>. The land is flat in most places. </p>
