      <p><b>Papua New Guinea</b> is an <a href="/wiki/Island" title="Island">island</a> country located on the Pacific Ocean. It is the east half of <a href="/wiki/New_Guinea" title="New Guinea">New Guinea</a> <a href="/wiki/Island" title="Island">island</a>, plus some nearby islands. The <a href="/wiki/Capital_city" title="Capital city">capital city</a> of Papua New Guinea is <a href="/wiki/Port_Moresby" title="Port Moresby">Port Moresby</a>. The population of Papua New Guinea are mostly the <a href="/wiki/Indigenous_peoples" title="Indigenous peoples">Indigenous peoples</a> of the island. </p>
      <p>The island is in both <a href="/wiki/Australasia" title="Australasia">Australasia</a> and <a href="/wiki/Oceania" title="Oceania">Oceania</a>, which are two different terms for the <a href="/wiki/Continent" title="Continent">continent</a> of islands in the <a href="/wiki/Pacific" class="mw-redirect" title="Pacific">Pacific</a> area. It borders <a href="/wiki/Indonesia" title="Indonesia">Indonesia</a> to the west and near <a href="/wiki/Australia" title="Australia">Australia</a> to the south. </p>

      <h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Administrative_divisions">Administrative divisions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Papua_New_Guinea&amp;veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Change section: Administrative divisions">change</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Papua_New_Guinea&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Change section: Administrative divisions">change source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3> 
      <p>Papua New Guinea is divided into four regions. These regions are important for government, commercial, sporting and other activities. </p>
      <p>The nation has 20 <a href="/wiki/Province" title="Province">province</a>-level divisions: eighteen, the Autonomous Region of <a href="/w/index.php?title=Bougainville_Province&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Bougainville Province (not yet started)">Bougainville</a> and the <a href="/w/index.php?title=National_Capital_District_(Papua_New_Guinea)&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="National Capital District (Papua New Guinea) (not yet started)">National Capital District</a>. Each province is divided into one or more districts. The districts are divided into one or more Local Level Government areas. </p>
