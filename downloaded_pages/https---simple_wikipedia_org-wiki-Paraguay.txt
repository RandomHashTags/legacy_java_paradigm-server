      <p><b>Paraguay</b>, officially the <b>Republic of Paraguay</b> (<a href="/wiki/Spanish_language" title="Spanish language">Spanish</a>: <i lang="es">República del Paraguay</i>), is a small country in <a href="/wiki/South_America" title="South America">South America</a>. It is <a href="/wiki/Landlocked" class="mw-redirect" title="Landlocked">landlocked</a>, meaning that it does not touch the ocean. It is bordered to the North and East by Brazil, the West by Bolivia, and the South and Southeast by Argentina. The main <a href="/wiki/Languages" class="mw-redirect" title="Languages">languages</a> are <a href="/wiki/Spanish_language" title="Spanish language">Spanish</a> and <a href="/wiki/Guaran%C3%AD_language" title="Guaraní language">Guaraní</a>. Paraguay's population is about 6 million. The ethnic makeup of the population is like the following: <a href="/wiki/Mestizo" title="Mestizo">Mestizo</a> (mixed European and Amerindian) 80%, European 20%, unmixed Amerindian 1-3%, Asian 1-4%. </p>
      <p>Over (20%) of the population lives below the poverty line. </p>
      <p>Paraguay has two official languages, <a href="/wiki/Spanish_language" title="Spanish language">Spanish</a> and Guarani. Guaraní is recognized as a national language. </p>
      <p>The capital city is <a href="/wiki/Asuncion" class="mw-redirect" title="Asuncion">Asuncion</a>. Asuncion was founded in <a href="/wiki/1537" title="1537">1537</a> by a man from <a href="/wiki/Spain" title="Spain">Spain</a> named <a href="/w/index.php?title=Juan_de_Salazar&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Juan de Salazar (not yet started)">Juan de Salazar</a>. Paraguay was a <a href="/wiki/Colony" title="Colony">colony</a> of Spain. Paraguay became <a href="/wiki/Independent" class="mw-redirect" title="Independent">independent</a> from Spain in <a href="/wiki/1811" title="1811">1811</a>. </p>
      <p>From <a href="/wiki/1865" title="1865">1865</a>-<a href="/wiki/1870" title="1870">1870</a> Paraguay lost land and most of its male population in the <a href="/w/index.php?title=War_of_the_Triple_Alliance&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="War of the Triple Alliance (not yet started)">War of the Triple Alliance</a> against <a href="/wiki/Brazil" title="Brazil">Brazil</a>, <a href="/wiki/Argentina" title="Argentina">Argentina</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Uruguay" title="Uruguay">Uruguay</a>. It gained land in the <a href="/w/index.php?title=Chaco_War&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Chaco War (not yet started)">Chaco War</a> against <a href="/wiki/Bolivia" title="Bolivia">Bolivia</a> in the 1930s. </p>
      <p>Paraguay's religion is Roman Catholicism. </p>
      <p>The weather is more humid in the east and dryer in the west. </p> 
      <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Government_and_politics">Government and politics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Paraguay&amp;veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Change section: Government and politics">change</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Paraguay&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Change section: Government and politics">change source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
      <h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Administrative_subdivisions">Administrative subdivisions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Paraguay&amp;veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Change section: Administrative subdivisions">change</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Paraguay&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Change section: Administrative subdivisions">change source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3> 
      <p>Paraguay has 17 <a href="/wiki/Departments_of_Paraguay" title="Departments of Paraguay">departments</a> and one <a href="/wiki/Capital_city" title="Capital city">capital</a> <a href="/wiki/District" title="District">district</a> (<i>Distrito Capital</i>). The departments are formed by districts. </p>
      <p>The departments are grouped in two <a href="/wiki/Geography" title="Geography">geographic</a> regions, separated by the <a href="/wiki/Paraguay_River" title="Paraguay River">Paraguay</a> <a href="/wiki/River" title="River">river</a>: </p> 
      <ul>
       <li><b>Occidental</b> (Western) region or <b>Chaco</b>, to the north of the Paraguay river (departments: Alto Paraguay, Boquerón and Presidente Hayes)</li> 
       <li><b>Oriental</b> (Eastern) region, to the south of the Paraguay river (departments: Alto Paraná, Amambay, Caaguazú, Caazapá, Canindeyú, Central, Concepción, Cordillera, Guairá, Itapúa, Misiones, Paraguarí, San Pedro and Ñeembucú; the capital district is part of this region)</li>
      </ul>
