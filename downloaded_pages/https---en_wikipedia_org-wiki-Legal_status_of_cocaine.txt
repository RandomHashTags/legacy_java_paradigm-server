         <td>Cocaine was first made illegal by the <a href="/wiki/Dangerous_Drugs_Act_1920" title="Dangerous Drugs Act 1920">Dangerous Drugs Act 1920</a>. It is now classed as a <a href="/wiki/Class_A_drug" class="mw-redirect" title="Class A drug">Class A drug</a>, controlled by the <a href="/wiki/Misuse_of_Drugs_Act_1971" title="Misuse of Drugs Act 1971">Misuse of Drugs Act 1971</a>. Possession carries a punishment of up to 7 years in prison, an unlimited fine or both. Supply and production carries a punishment of up to life in prison, an unlimited fine or both.<sup id="cite_ref-21" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-21">[21]</a></sup> It is legal for medical use as a local anaesthetic. </td>
