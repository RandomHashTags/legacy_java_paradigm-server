      <p>This is a <b>list of countries by <a href="/wiki/Papaya" title="Papaya">papaya</a> production</b> from the years 2016 to 2018, based on data from the <a href="/wiki/Food_and_Agriculture_Organization_Corporate_Statistical_Database" title="Food and Agriculture Organization Corporate Statistical Database">Food and Agriculture Organization Corporate Statistical Database</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The estimated total world production for papayas in 2018 was 13,290,320 <a href="/wiki/Tonne" title="Tonne">metric tonnes</a>, up by 2.5% from 12,971,418 tonnes in 2017.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[n 1]</a></sup> India was by far the largest producer, accounting for 45% of global production. Dependent territories are shown in italics. </p>
