      <p>This is a <b>list of countries by <a href="/wiki/Grape" title="Grape">grape</a> production</b> from the years 2016 to 2018, based on data from the <a href="/wiki/Food_and_Agriculture_Organization_Corporate_Statistical_Database" title="Food and Agriculture Organization Corporate Statistical Database">Food and Agriculture Organization Corporate Statistical Database</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The estimated total world production for grapes in 2018 was 79,125,982 <a href="/wiki/Tonne" title="Tonne">metric tonnes</a>, up by 6.5% from 74,276,583 tonnes in 2017.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[n 1]</a></sup> China was the largest producer of grapes, accounting for 16.9% of global production. Italy came second at 10.8%, followed by the United States at 8.7%. </p>
