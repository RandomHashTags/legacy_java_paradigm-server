      <p>This is a <b>list of countries by <a href="/wiki/Plum" title="Plum">plum</a> and <a href="/wiki/Prunus_spinosa" title="Prunus spinosa">sloe</a> production</b> in 2016 and 2017, based on data from the <a href="/wiki/Food_and_Agriculture_Organization_Corporate_Statistical_Database" title="Food and Agriculture Organization Corporate Statistical Database">Food and Agriculture Organization Corporate Statistical Database</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The estimated total world production of plum and sloe for 2017 was 11,758,135 <a href="/wiki/Tonne" title="Tonne">metric tonnes</a>, down 1% from 11,875,874 tonnes in 2016.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[n 1]</a></sup> China was by far the largest producer, accounting for nearly 58% of global production. </p>
