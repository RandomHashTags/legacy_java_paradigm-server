      <p>The <b>United Arab Emirates</b> or the <b>UAE</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Country" title="Country">country</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Middle_East" title="Middle East">Middle East</a>. It is made up of seven <a href="/wiki/Emirate" title="Emirate">emirates</a> located in the north-eastern part of the <a href="/wiki/Arabian_Peninsula" title="Arabian Peninsula">Arabian Peninsula</a>, on the south side of the <a href="/wiki/Persian_Gulf" title="Persian Gulf">Persian Gulf</a>. </p>

      <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Names">Names</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=United_Arab_Emirates&amp;veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Change section: Names">change</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/w/index.php?title=United_Arab_Emirates&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Change section: Names">change source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
      <p>People from this country are called Emiratis or Emirati citizens. Emirians used to be called Trucial Coasters or just Trucials before independence. Emirians from ancient history are called <a href="/wiki/Maganite" class="mw-redirect" title="Maganite">Maganites</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> </p> 
      <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=United_Arab_Emirates&amp;veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Change section: Geography">change</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/w/index.php?title=United_Arab_Emirates&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Change section: Geography">change source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
      <p>It borders <a href="/wiki/Saudi_Arabia" title="Saudi Arabia">Saudi Arabia</a> to the south, <a href="/wiki/Oman" title="Oman">Oman</a> to the east, and <a href="/wiki/Qatar" title="Qatar">Qatar</a> to the west. The <a href="/wiki/Capital_city" title="Capital city">capital city</a> of the UAE is <a href="/wiki/Abu_Dhabi" title="Abu Dhabi">Abu Dhabi</a>, and the biggest city is <a href="/wiki/Dubai" title="Dubai">Dubai</a>. These cities have become <a href="/wiki/Global_city" title="Global city">global cities</a> and are often visited by <a href="/wiki/Tourism" title="Tourism">tourists</a>. They have many <a href="/wiki/Skyscraper" title="Skyscraper">skyscrapers</a> and one of them, the <a href="/wiki/Burj_Khalifa" title="Burj Khalifa">Burj Khalifa</a>, is the world's tallest structure.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> </p>

      <p>As a member of the <a href="/wiki/Arab_League" title="Arab League">Arab League</a>, the country's official language is <a href="/wiki/Arabic_language" title="Arabic language">Arabic</a>. The country has some <a href="/wiki/Tradition" class="mw-redirect" title="Tradition">traditions</a> and one of them is eating <a href="/wiki/Date_(fruit)" class="mw-redirect" title="Date (fruit)">dates</a>. That is why <a href="/wiki/Date_Palm" class="mw-redirect" title="Date Palm">date palms</a> are often grown in the <a href="/wiki/UAE" class="mw-redirect" title="UAE">UAE</a>. </p> 
      <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="The_seven_emirates_of_the_UAE">The seven emirates of the UAE</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=United_Arab_Emirates&amp;veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Change section: The seven emirates of the UAE">change</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/w/index.php?title=United_Arab_Emirates&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Change section: The seven emirates of the UAE">change source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
      <p>The seven emirates (territories which have an <a href="/wiki/Islam" title="Islam">Islamic</a> <a href="/wiki/Emir" title="Emir">emir</a> as its <a href="/wiki/Monarch" title="Monarch">monarch</a> and <a href="/wiki/Head_of_state" title="Head of state">head of state</a>) which make up the UAE used to have a special <a href="/wiki/Peace_treaty" title="Peace treaty">peace treaty</a> (or <i>truce</i>) with the <a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom" title="United Kingdom">United Kingdom</a>. This is why their old name was the Trucial States. These are: </p>
