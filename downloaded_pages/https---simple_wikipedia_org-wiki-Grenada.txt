      <p><b>Grenada</b> (<span class="rt-commentedText nowrap"><span class="IPA nopopups noexcerpt"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA/English" title="Help:IPA/English">/<span style="border-bottom:1px dotted"><span title="/ɡ/: 'g' in 'guy'">ɡ</span><span title="'r' in 'rye'">r</span><span title="/ə/: 'a' in 'about'">ə</span><span title="/ˈ/: primary stress follows">ˈ</span><span title="'n' in 'nigh'">n</span><span title="/eɪ/: 'a' in 'face'">eɪ</span><span title="'d' in 'dye'">d</span><span title="/ə/: 'a' in 'about'">ə</span></span>/</a></span> <span class="nowrap" style="font-size:85%">(<span class="unicode haudio"><span class="fn"><span style="white-space:nowrap;margin-right:.25em;"><a href="/wiki/File:En-us-Grenada.ogg" title="About this sound"><img alt="About this sound" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/8a/Loudspeaker.svg/11px-Loudspeaker.svg.png" decoding="async" width="11" height="11" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/8a/Loudspeaker.svg/17px-Loudspeaker.svg.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/8a/Loudspeaker.svg/22px-Loudspeaker.svg.png 2x" data-file-width="20" data-file-height="20"></a></span><a href="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/c/c0/En-us-Grenada.ogg" class="internal" title="En-us-Grenada.ogg">listen</a></span></span>)</span></span> <a href="/wiki/Help:Pronunciation_respelling_key" title="Help:Pronunciation respelling key"><i title="English pronunciation respelling">grə-<span style="font-size:90%">NAY</span>-də</i></a>; <a href="/w/index.php?title=Grenadian_Creole_French&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Grenadian Creole French (not yet started)">Grenadian Creole French</a>: <i>Gwenad</i>) is an island nation in the <a href="/wiki/Caribbean_Sea" title="Caribbean Sea">Caribbean Sea</a> that received its <a href="/wiki/Independence" title="Independence">independence</a> from the <a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom" title="United Kingdom">United Kingdom</a> in 1974. Its capital is <a href="/wiki/St._George%27s,_Grenada" title="St. George's, Grenada">St. George's</a>. </p>
      <p>The island was invaded by the <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">American</a> military in 1983 because of <a href="/wiki/Cuba" title="Cuba">Cuba</a>'s presence there. In 2004, a hurricane named <a href="/wiki/Hurricane_Ivan" title="Hurricane Ivan">Ivan</a> destroyed most of the island's resources. </p>
      <p>Grenada is called the <b><a href="/wiki/Spice" title="Spice">Spice</a> Island</b> on account of its most famous export, the <a href="/wiki/Nutmeg" title="Nutmeg">nutmeg</a>. </p>
