      <p>The <b>Sahrawi Arab Democratic Republic</b> (<b>SADR</b>) (<a href="/wiki/Arabic_language" title="Arabic language">Arabic</a>: <span lang="ar" dir="rtl"><b>الجمهورية العربية الصحراوية الديمقراطية</b></span>‎, <a href="/wiki/Spanish_language" title="Spanish language">Spanish</a>: <i lang="es">República Árabe Saharaui Democrática</i>) is a [[Legal status of Western Sahara<a href="/w/index.php?title=Its_recognized_by_the_African_union&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Its recognized by the African union (not yet started)">its recognized by the African union </a> that claims sovereignty over the entire territory of <a href="/wiki/Western_Sahara" title="Western Sahara">Western Sahara</a>. SADR was proclaimed by the <a href="/wiki/Polisario_Front" title="Polisario Front">Polisario Front</a> on February 27, 1976 in <a href="/w/index.php?title=Bir_Lehlu&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Bir Lehlu (not yet started)">Bir Lehlu</a>, Western Sahara. The SADR government currently controls between 20% and 25% of the <a href="/wiki/Territory_(country_subdivision)" class="mw-redirect" title="Territory (country subdivision)">territory</a> it claims. It calls the territories under its control the Liberated Territories or <a href="/w/index.php?title=Free_Zone_(Western_Sahara_region)&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Free Zone (Western Sahara region) (not yet started)">Free Zone</a>. <a href="/wiki/Morocco" title="Morocco">Morocco</a> controls and administers the rest of the disputed territory and calls these lands its <a href="/w/index.php?title=Southern_Provinces&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Southern Provinces (not yet started)">Southern Provinces</a>. The SADR government considers the Moroccan-held territory occupied territory, while Morocco considers the much smaller SADR held territory to be a buffer zone. </p>
      <p>The <a href="/wiki/United_Nations" title="United Nations">United Nations</a> calls all of the <a href="/wiki/Western_Sahara" title="Western Sahara">Western Sahara</a> a <a href="/wiki/Dependent_territory" title="Dependent territory">dependency</a> of <a href="/wiki/Spain" title="Spain">Spain</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> </p>
