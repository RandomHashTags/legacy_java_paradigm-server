      <p><b>Guinea</b>, or the <b>Republic of Guinea</b> (<a href="/wiki/French_language" title="French language">French</a>: <i lang="fr">République de Guinée</i>), is an independent nation in western <a href="/wiki/Africa" title="Africa">Africa</a>. They speak French. It's a member of Economic Community of West African States. </p>

      <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Prefectures_and_regions">Prefectures and regions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Guinea&amp;veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Change section: Prefectures and regions">change</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Guinea&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Change section: Prefectures and regions">change source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>

The capital of Guinea, Conakry, is a special area. </p>
