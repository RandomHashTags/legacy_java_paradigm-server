'moral'">ɒr</span><span title="/ə/: 'a' in 'about'">ə</span></span>/</a></span> <span class="nowrap" style="font-size:85%">(<span class="unicode haudio"><span class="fn"><span href="/wiki/List_of_countries" title="List of countries">sovereign country</a> located in the eastern <a href="/w/index.php?title=Pyrenees_Mountains&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" are about 84,000 people living in the country. The <a href="/wiki/Capital_city" title="Capital city">capital</a> is <a href="/wiki/Andorra_la_Vella" title="Andorra la Vella">Andorra la Vella</a>. It is ruled by a Spanish <a href="/wiki/Bishop" title="Bishop">Bishop</a> and the <a href="/wiki/President" title="President">French President</a>.       <p>Andorra is a rich country mostly because of tourism. There are about 10.2&nbsp;million visitors each year.<sup id="cite_ref-es2_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-es2-4">[4]</a></sup> </p>
      <p>The official language is <a href="/wiki/Catalan_language" title="Catalan language">Catalan</a>, although Spanish, Portuguese, and French are also used. </p>
      <p>It is not a member of the <a href="/wiki/European_Union" title="European Union">European</a> Union. The <a href="/wiki/Euro" title="Euro">euro</a> is the money used. </p>

      <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Andorra&amp;veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Change section: History">change</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Andorra&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Change section: History">change source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
      <p>It is said that Charles the Great (<a href="/wiki/Charlemagne" title="Charlemagne">Charlemagne</a>) gave the Andorran people their country in return for fighting against the <a href="/wiki/Moors" title="Moors">Moors</a>. </p>
      <p>Before 1095, Andorra did not have any type of military protection. The Lord of Caboet and the Bishop of Urgell decided to rule Andorra together. In 1607 <a href="/wiki/Henry_IV_of_France" title="Henry IV of France">Henry IV of France</a> said the head of the French state and the Bishop of Urgell would be co-princes of Andorra. </p>
      <p>Andorra declared war on <a href="/wiki/Imperial_Germany" class="mw-redirect" title="Imperial Germany">Imperial Germany</a> during <a href="/wiki/World_War_I" title="World War I">World War I</a>. It did not do any fighting. They stayed against Germany until 1957 because they were not in the <a href="/wiki/Treaty_of_Versailles" title="Treaty of Versailles">Treaty of Versailles</a>. </p>
      <p>Andorra doesn't have an Army.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> France and Spain help to defend Andorra. The country has a police force of 295. </p>

      <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Religion">Religion</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Andorra&amp;veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Change section: Religion">change</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Andorra&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Change section: Religion">change source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
      <p>The population of Andorra is mostly (90%) <a href="/wiki/Catholicism" title="Catholicism">Roman Catholic</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> Their patron saint is <a href="/wiki/Our_Lady_of_Meritxell" title="Our Lady of Meritxell">Our Lady of Meritxell</a>. </p> 
      <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Education">Education</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Andorra&amp;veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Change section: Education">change</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Andorra&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Change section: Education">change source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
      <p>Children between the ages of 6 and 16 must have full-time education. Education up to secondary level is paid by the government. </p>
      <p>The <a href="/wiki/University_of_Andorra" class="mw-redirect" title="University of Andorra">University of Andorra</a> (UdA) is the state public university. It is the only university in Andorra. </p>
