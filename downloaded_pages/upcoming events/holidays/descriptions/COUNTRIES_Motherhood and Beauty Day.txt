      <p><b>Motherhood and beauty day</b> (<a href="/wiki/Armenian_language" title="Armenian language">Armenian</a>: <span lang="hy">Մայրության և գեղեցկության օր</span>) is an official holiday in <a href="/wiki/Armenia" title="Armenia">Armenia</a> dedicated to women. While March 8 celebrates all women, April 7 is mother's day.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> April 7 is the <a href="/wiki/Feast_of_the_Annunciation" title="Feast of the Annunciation">Feast of the Annunciation</a> in the calendar of the <a href="/wiki/Armenian_Apostolic_Church" title="Armenian Apostolic Church">Armenian Apostolic Church</a>. It is expected to give presents to one's own <a href="/wiki/Mother" title="Mother">mother</a>. Celebrating each woman as beautiful in her own way, mothers are particularly happy to receive <a href="/wiki/Flowers" class="mw-redirect" title="Flowers">flowers</a>. </p>
