      <p><b>World Bee Day</b> is <a href="/wiki/List_of_minor_secular_observances#May" title="List of minor secular observances">celebrated</a> on May 20. On this day <a href="/wiki/Anton_Jan%C5%A1a" title="Anton Janša">Anton Janša</a>, the pioneer of <a href="/wiki/Beekeeping" title="Beekeeping">beekeeping</a>, was born in 1734. </p>
      <p>The purpose of the international day is to acknowledge the role of <a href="/wiki/Bee" title="Bee">bees</a> and other <a href="/wiki/Pollinators" class="mw-redirect" title="Pollinators">pollinators</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Ecosystem" title="Ecosystem">ecosystem</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> </p>
      <p>The <a href="/wiki/UN" class="mw-redirect" title="UN">UN</a> Member States approved <a href="/wiki/Slovenia" title="Slovenia">Slovenia</a>’s proposal to proclaim 20 May as World Bee Day in December 2017.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> </p>
