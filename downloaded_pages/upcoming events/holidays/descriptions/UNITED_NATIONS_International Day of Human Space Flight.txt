   <h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading">International Day of Human Space Flight</h1> 
   <div id="bodyContent" class="mw-body-content"> 
    <div id="siteSub" class="noprint">

      <p>The <b>International Day of Human Space Flight</b> is the <a href="/wiki/List_of_minor_secular_observances#April" title="List of minor secular observances">annual celebration</a>, held on 12 April, of the anniversary of the first human space flight by <a href="/wiki/Yuri_Gagarin" title="Yuri Gagarin">Yuri Gagarin</a> (<a href="/wiki/Soviet_Union" title="Soviet Union">USSR</a>). It was proclaimed at the 65th session of the <a href="/wiki/United_Nations_General_Assembly" title="United Nations General Assembly">United Nations General Assembly</a> on 7 April 2011,<sup id="cite_ref-UN_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-UN-1">[1]</a></sup> a few days before the 50th anniversary of the flight. </p>
      <p>Yuri Gagarin crewed the <a href="/wiki/Vostok_1" title="Vostok 1">Vostok 1</a> mission in 1961, completing one orbit around Earth over 108 minutes in the <a href="/wiki/Vostok_3KA" class="mw-redirect" title="Vostok 3KA">Vostok 3KA</a> spacecraft, launched on a <a href="/wiki/Vostok-K" title="Vostok-K">Vostok-K</a> rocket from <a href="/wiki/Baikonur_Cosmodrome" title="Baikonur Cosmodrome">Baikonur Cosmodrome</a> in Kazakhstan, USSR. </p>
      <p>In the Soviet Union, 12 April was commemorated as <a href="/wiki/Cosmonautics_Day" title="Cosmonautics Day">Cosmonautics Day</a> since 1963, and is still observed in Russia and some <a href="/wiki/Former_Soviet_state" class="mw-redirect" title="Former Soviet state">former Soviet states</a>. <a href="/wiki/Yuri%27s_Night" title="Yuri's Night">Yuri's Night</a>, also known as "World Space Party" is an international observance initiated in the <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a> in 2001, on the 40th anniversary of Gagarin's flight. </p>
      <p>Also commemorated on this date is the first <a href="/wiki/Space_Shuttle" title="Space Shuttle">Space Shuttle</a> launch, <a href="/wiki/STS-1" title="STS-1">STS-1</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Space_Shuttle_Columbia" title="Space Shuttle Columbia">Columbia</a></i> on 12 April 1981, exactly 20 years after the first human spaceflight. </p>
