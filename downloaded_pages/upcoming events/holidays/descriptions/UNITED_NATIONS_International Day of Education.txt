   <h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading">International Day of Education</h1>

      <p><b>International Day of Education</b> is an annual <a href="/wiki/List_of_minor_secular_observances#January" title="List of minor secular observances">international observance day</a> held on January 24 and is dedicated to <a href="/wiki/Education" title="Education">education</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> On December 3, 2018, the <a href="/wiki/United_Nations_General_Assembly" title="United Nations General Assembly">United Nations General Assembly</a> adopted a resolution proclaiming January 24 as <b>International Day of Education</b>, in celebration of the role of <a href="/wiki/Education" title="Education">education</a> for bringing <a href="/wiki/Global_peace" class="mw-redirect" title="Global peace">global peace</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sustainable_development" title="Sustainable development">sustainable development</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> </p>

      <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=International_Day_of_Education&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
      <p>24th January was declared the international Day of Education by a resolution that was passed by the United Nations General Assembly (UNGA) on 3rd December 2018. Thereafter, on 24th January 2019 the first International Day of Education was celebrated. The message by UNGA is being spread across globe. Their sincere efforts world wide have shown promising results in the betterment of an educated individual that constitute a cultured society, which is supported with optimism and opportunities.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> </p>
