      <p class="mw-empty-elt"> </p>
      <p><b>Constitution Day</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Holiday" title="Holiday">holiday</a> to honor the <a href="/wiki/Constitution" title="Constitution">constitution</a> of a country. Constitution Day is often celebrated on the <a href="/wiki/Anniversary" title="Anniversary">anniversary</a> of the signing, <a href="/wiki/Promulgation" title="Promulgation">promulgation</a> or adoption of the constitution, or in some cases, to commemorate the change to <a href="/wiki/Constitutional_monarchy" title="Constitutional monarchy">constitutional monarchy</a>. </p>
