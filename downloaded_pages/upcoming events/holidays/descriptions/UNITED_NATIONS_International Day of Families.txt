      <div role="note" class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable">
       This article is about the International Day of Families of the UN General Assembly. For the International Meeting of Families of the Catholic Church, see <a href="/wiki/World_Meeting_of_Families" title="World Meeting of Families">World Meeting of Families</a>.
      </div> 
      <p>The <b>International Day of Families</b> is <a href="/wiki/List_of_minor_secular_observances#May" title="List of minor secular observances">observed</a> on the <a href="/wiki/May_15" title="May 15">15 May</a> every year. The Day was proclaimed by the UN General Assembly in 1993 with resolution A/RES/47/237 and reflects the importance the <a href="/wiki/International_community" title="International community">international community</a> attaches to <a href="/wiki/Family" title="Family">families</a>. The International Day provides an opportunity to <a href="/wiki/Raising_awareness" class="mw-redirect" title="Raising awareness">promote awareness</a> of issues relating to families and to increase knowledge of the social, economic and demographic processes affecting families.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> </p> 
      <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Themes">Themes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=International_Day_of_Families&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Themes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
      <p>Every year, the UN secretary-general makes to the particular applicable motto attention. This was from 1996:<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> </p> 
      <ul>
       <li>2020- “Families in Development: <a href="/wiki/World_Conference_on_Women,_1980" title="World Conference on Women, 1980">Copenhagen</a> &amp; <a href="/wiki/Beijing_Declaration" title="Beijing Declaration">Beijing</a> + 25”<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></li>
      </ul> 
      <ul>
       <li>2019 - "Families and Climate Action: Focus on <a href="/wiki/Sustainable_Development_Goals" title="Sustainable Development Goals">SDG</a> 13"<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></li> 
       <li>2018 - "Families and inclusive societies"<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></li> 
       <li>2017 - "Families, education and <a href="/wiki/Well-being" title="Well-being">well-being</a>"<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></li> 
       <li>2016 - "Families, healthy lives and <a href="/wiki/Sustainability" title="Sustainability">sustainable</a> future"</li> 
       <li>2015 - "Men in charge? <a href="/wiki/Gender_equality" title="Gender equality">Gender equality</a> and <a href="/wiki/Children%27s_rights" title="Children's rights">children's rights</a> in contemporary families"</li> 
       <li>2014 - "Families Matter for the Achievement of Development Goals; International Year of the Family + 20"</li> 
       <li>2013 - "Advancing <a href="/wiki/Social_integration" title="Social integration">Social Integration</a> and Intergenerational Solidarity"</li> 
       <li>2012 - "Ensuring work family balance"</li> 
       <li>2011 - "Confronting Family Poverty and <a href="/wiki/Social_exclusion" title="Social exclusion">Social Exclusion</a>"</li> 
       <li>2010 - "The impact of migration on families around the world"</li> 
       <li>2009 - "Mothers and Families: Challenges in a Changing World"</li> 
       <li>2008 - "Fathers and Families: Responsibilities and Challenges"</li> 
       <li>2007 - "Families and Persons with <a href="/wiki/Disability" title="Disability">Disabilities</a>"</li> 
       <li>2006 - "Changing Families: Challenges and Opportunities"</li> 
       <li>2005 - "HIV/AIDS and Family Well-being"</li> 
       <li>2004 - "The Tenth Anniversary of the International Year of the Family: A Framework for Action"</li> 
       <li>2003 - "Preparations for the observance of the Tenth Anniversary of the International Year of the Family in 2004"</li> 
       <li>2002 - "Families and Ageing: Opportunities and Challenges"</li> 
       <li>2001 - "Families and Volunteers: Building Social Cohesion "</li> 
       <li>2000 - "Families: Agents and Beneficiaries of Development"</li> 
       <li>1999 - "Families for all ages"</li> 
       <li>1998 - "Families: Educators and Providers of Human Rights"</li> 
       <li>1997 - "Building Families Based on Partnership"</li> 
       <li>1996 - "Families: First Victims of Poverty and <a href="/wiki/Homelessness" title="Homelessness">Homelessness</a>"</li>
      </ul>
