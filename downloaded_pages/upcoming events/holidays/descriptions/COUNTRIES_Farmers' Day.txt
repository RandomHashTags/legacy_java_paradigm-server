      <p><b>Farmers' Day</b> is an annual observance in various countries to celebrate the national contributions of <a href="/wiki/Farmer" title="Farmer">farmers</a> and agriculturers. In India it is observed on December 23<i>.</i> </p>

      <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="List_by_country">List by country</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Farmers%27_Day&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: List by country">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
      <h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Ghana">Ghana</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Farmers%27_Day&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Ghana">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3> 
      <div role="note" class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable">
       Main article: <a href="/wiki/Farmers%27_Day_(Ghana)" class="mw-redirect" title="Farmers' Day (Ghana)">Farmers' Day (Ghana)</a>
      </div> 
      <p>The National Farmers' Day in <a href="/wiki/Ghana" title="Ghana">Ghana</a> is an annual celebration of farmers and fishermen, observed on the first Friday of December. On Farmers' Day, the <a href="/wiki/Ministry_of_Food_and_Agriculture_(Ghana)" title="Ministry of Food and Agriculture (Ghana)">Ministry of Food and Agriculture (Ghana)</a> honors with special awards to deserving farmers and fishermen based on their practices and output.<sup id="cite_ref-Ghana_Ministry_of_Food_and_Agriculture_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Ghana_Ministry_of_Food_and_Agriculture-1">[1]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> </p> 
      <h3><span class="mw-headline" id="India">India</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Farmers%27_Day&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: India">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3> 
      <p>The National Farmers Day in India is also known as <b>Kisan Divas</b> in Hindi.<sup id="cite_ref-statetimes_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-statetimes-3">[3]</a></sup> Farmer's Day is celebrated every year on 23 December,<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> on the birthday of the 5th <a href="/wiki/Prime_Minister_of_India" title="Prime Minister of India">Prime Minister of India</a>, <a href="/wiki/Choudhary_Charan_Singh" class="mw-redirect" title="Choudhary Charan Singh">Choudhary Charan Singh</a>, also a farmer's leader, who introduced many policies to improve the lives of the Indian farmers.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> It is celebrated by organising various programs, debates, seminars, quiz competitions, discussions, workshops, exhibitions, essays writing competitions and functions.<sup id="cite_ref-statetimes_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-statetimes-3">[3]</a></sup> </p> 
      <h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Pakistan">Pakistan</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Farmers%27_Day&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Pakistan">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3> 
      <p>The National Farmers' Day in Pakistan, also known as <b>Kissan Day</b>,<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> was celebrated for the first time in the country's history on December 18, 2019 in Islamabad as was also acknowledged by the Prime Minister <a href="/wiki/Imran_Khan" title="Imran Khan">Imran Khan</a>. The idea was proposed by Pakistan's leading Fertilizer manufacturing company Fatima Group in favor of promoting farmer welfare and prosperity while celebrating Pakistan's first farmers' day on December 18, 2019 during a special event in Islamabad which was acknowledged and endorsed by the Country's premier.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> </p> 
      <h3><span class="mw-headline" id="South_Korea">South Korea</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Farmers%27_Day&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: South Korea">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3> 
      <p>In South Korea, the observance is on 11 November. It competes with the <a href="/wiki/Pepero_Day" title="Pepero Day">Pepero Day</a> on the same day. </p> 
      <h3><span class="mw-headline" id="United_States">United States</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Farmers%27_Day&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: United States">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3> 
      <p>In America, it is observed on 12 October every year. It is celebrated to pay tribute to all farmers throughout <a href="/wiki/American_history" class="mw-redirect" title="American history">American history</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup> </p> 
      <h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Zambia">Zambia</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Farmers%27_Day&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Zambia">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3> 
      <div role="note" class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable">
       Main article: <a href="/wiki/Farmers%27_Day_(Zambia)" class="mw-redirect" title="Farmers' Day (Zambia)">Farmers' Day (Zambia)</a>
      </div> 
      <p>The National Farmers' Day in <a href="/wiki/Zambia" title="Zambia">Zambia</a> is observed on the first Monday Of August.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup> </p>
