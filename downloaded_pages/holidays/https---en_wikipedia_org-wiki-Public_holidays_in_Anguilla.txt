      <p> <b>Holidays in Anguilla</b> are predominantly religious holidays, with a number of additional national holidays. The most important holiday in the Territory is Separation day, which celebrates the separation of the island from <a href="/wiki/Saint_Kitts_and_Nevis" title="Saint Kitts and Nevis">Saint Kitts and Nevis</a>. </p>
      <p>Where fixed date holidays (such as Christmas Day and Boxing Day) fall on a <a href="/wiki/Workweek" class="mw-redirect" title="Workweek">weekend</a>, the holiday is taken in lieu on the next succeeding working day. </p>
