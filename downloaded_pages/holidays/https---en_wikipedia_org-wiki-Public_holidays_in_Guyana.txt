      <p>Since 1957, September has been designated <a href="/wiki/Amerindian_Heritage_Month_(Guyana)" title="Amerindian Heritage Month (Guyana)">Amerindian Heritage Month</a>. September 10 is considered Heritage Day, marking Guyana's first Amerindian <a href="/wiki/Member_of_Parliament" class="mw-redirect" title="Member of Parliament">Member of Parliament</a>. </p>
