      <p>All official <a href="/wiki/Holiday" title="Holiday">holidays</a> in <a href="/wiki/Estonia" title="Estonia">Estonia</a> are established by acts of <a href="/wiki/Parliament_of_Estonia" class="mw-redirect" title="Parliament of Estonia">Parliament</a>. </p>
