      <p>This is a list of <b><a href="/wiki/Public_holiday" title="Public holiday">holidays</a> in <a href="/wiki/Tunisia" title="Tunisia">Tunisia</a></b>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> </p> 
      <ul>
       <li>January 1: <a href="/wiki/New_Year%27s_Day" title="New Year's Day">New Year's Day</a></li> 
       <li>January 14: Revolution and Youth Day</li> 
       <li>March 20: Independence Day<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></li> 
       <li>April 9: Martyrs' Day<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></li> 
       <li>May 1: Labour Day</li> 
       <li>July 25: Republic Day<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></li> 
       <li>August 13: Women's Day</li> 
       <li>October 15: Evacuation Day</li> 
       <li><a href="/wiki/Eid_al-Fitr" title="Eid al-Fitr">Eid al-Fitr</a> (End of Ramadan) (depending on the <a href="/wiki/Lunar_calendar" title="Lunar calendar">moon calendar</a>, shifts towards the beginning of the year by 11 days per year)</li> 
       <li><a href="/wiki/Eid_al-Adha" title="Eid al-Adha">Eid al-Adha</a> (Feast of the Sacrifice) (depending on the moon calendar, shifts towards the beginning of the year by 11 days per year)</li> 
       <li>Hegire (Islamic New Year) (depending on the moon calendar, shifts towards the beginning of the year by 11 days per year)<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></li> 
       <li>Mouled (Prophet's Anniversary) (depending on the moon calendar, shifts towards the beginning of the year by 11 days per year)</li>
      </ul>
