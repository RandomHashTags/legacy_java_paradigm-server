      <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Public_holidays">Public holidays</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Public_holidays_in_Guatemala&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Public holidays">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
      <p>The Guatemalan Labor Code recognizes the following dates as public holidays with paid leave (unless marked as "Bank holiday"):<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> </p> 
      <table class="wikitable"> 
       <tbody>
        <tr> 
         <th>Date </th> 
         <th>English Name </th> 
         <th>Local Name </th> 
         <th>Remarks </th>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>January 1 </td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/New_Year%27s_Day" title="New Year's Day">New Year's Day</a> </td> 
         <td><i>Año Nuevo</i> </td> 
         <td>The celebration of the first day of the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_Calendar" class="mw-redirect" title="Gregorian Calendar">Gregorian Calendar</a>. </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>March or April </td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/Holy_Week" title="Holy Week">Holy Week</a> </td> 
         <td><i>Semana Santa</i><sup id="cite_ref-holidays_2020_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-holidays_2020-4">[4]</a></sup> </td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/Holy_Wednesday" title="Holy Wednesday">Holy Wednesday</a> is a Bank holiday. <a href="/wiki/Maundy_Thursday" title="Maundy Thursday">Maundy Thursday</a>, <a href="/wiki/Good_Friday" title="Good Friday">Good Friday</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Holy_Saturday" title="Holy Saturday">Holy Saturday</a> are official holidays. </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>May 1 </td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/Labour_Day" title="Labour Day">Labour Day</a> </td> 
         <td><i>Día del Trabajo</i> </td> 
         <td>Also called <a href="/wiki/International_Workers%27_Day" title="International Workers' Day">International Workers' Day</a> </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>May 10 </td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/Mother%27s_Day#By_country_(A–G)" title="Mother's Day">Mother's Day</a> </td> 
         <td><i>Día de la Madre</i> </td> 
         <td>Only observed by mothers </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>June 30 (variable) </td> 
         <td><a href="/w/index.php?title=Army_Day_(Guatemala)&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Army Day (Guatemala) (page does not exist)">Army Day</a> </td> 
         <td><i>Día del Ejército</i> </td> 
         <td> </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>July 1 </td> 
         <td>Bank Holiday </td> 
         <td><i>Día del Empleado Bancario</i> </td> 
         <td>The supervisory body in charge of the banking sector in Guatemala (<i>la Superintendencia de Bancos de Guatemala</i>) announced that bank holidays would be observed in Guatemala every year.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>August 15 </td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/Assumption_of_Mary" title="Assumption of Mary">Assumption Day</a> </td> 
         <td><i>Día de la Asunción</i> </td> 
         <td>Assumption of Mary into Heaven (only in <a href="/wiki/Guatemala_City" title="Guatemala City">Guatemala City</a>) </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>September 15 </td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/D%C3%ADas_Patrios_(Guatemala)" title="Días Patrios (Guatemala)">Independence Day</a> </td> 
         <td><i>Día de la Independencia</i> </td> 
         <td>Celebrates the <a href="/wiki/Act_of_Independence_of_Central_America" title="Act of Independence of Central America">Act of Independence of Central America</a> in 1821. </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>October 12 </td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/Columbus_Day#Día_de_la_Raza" title="Columbus Day">Day of the Race</a> </td> 
         <td><i>Día de la Raza</i> </td> 
         <td>Celebrates the resistance to the European conquest of America.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> Bank holiday. </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>October 20 </td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/Guatemalan_Revolution" title="Guatemalan Revolution">Revolution Day</a> </td> 
         <td><i>Día de la Revolución</i> </td> 
         <td>Celebrates the "Ten Years of Spring," the democratic period that began with the uprising against <a href="/wiki/Jorge_Ubico" title="Jorge Ubico">Jorge Ubico</a> in 1944. </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>November 1 </td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/All_Saints%27_Day" title="All Saints' Day">All Saints' Day</a> </td> 
         <td><i>Día de Todos los Santos</i> </td> 
         <td> </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>December 24 </td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/Christmas_Eve" title="Christmas Eve">Christmas Eve</a> </td> 
         <td><i>Noche Buena</i> </td> 
         <td>From noon onward (Bank holiday) </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>December 25 </td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/Christmas" title="Christmas">Christmas</a> </td> 
         <td><i>Navidad</i> </td> 
         <td> </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>December 31 </td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/New_Year%27s_Eve" title="New Year's Eve">New Year's Eve</a> </td> 
         <td><i>Víspera de Año Nuevo</i> </td> 
         <td>From noon onward (Bank holiday) </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>Variable </td> 
         <td>The local holiday </td> 
         <td> </td> 
         <td>Usually day of a patron saint. For example, Assumption Day is celebrated in Guatemala City on August 15. </td>
        </tr>
