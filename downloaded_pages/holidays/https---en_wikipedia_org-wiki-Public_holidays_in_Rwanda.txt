   <h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading" lang="en">Public holidays in Rwanda</h1>

      <p>This is a list of <b>public holidays in Rwanda</b>. <a href="/wiki/Rwanda" title="Rwanda">Rwanda</a> observes fourteen<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> regular <a href="/wiki/Public_holidays" class="mw-redirect" title="Public holidays">public holidays</a>. </p>
      <p>Additionally, the week following Genocide Memorial Day on 7 April is designated an official week of mourning.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> The last Saturday of each month is <i><a href="/wiki/Umuganda" title="Umuganda">umuganda</a></i>, a national day of <a href="/wiki/Community_service" title="Community service">community service</a>, during which most normal services remain closed until midday.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> </p>
