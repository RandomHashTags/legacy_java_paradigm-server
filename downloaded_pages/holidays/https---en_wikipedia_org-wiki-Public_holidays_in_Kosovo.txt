      <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Annotations">Annotations</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Public_holidays_in_Kosovo&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Annotations">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
      <div class="reflist" style="list-style-type: lower-alpha;"> 
       <div class="mw-references-wrap">
        <ol class="references"> 
         <li id="cite_note-status-1"><span class="mw-cite-backlink"><b><a href="#cite_ref-status_1-0">^</a></b></span> <span class="reference-text"> Kosovo is the subject of a territorial dispute between the <a href="/wiki/Kosovo" title="Kosovo">Republic of Kosovo</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Serbia" title="Serbia">Republic of Serbia</a>. The Republic of Kosovo <a href="/wiki/2008_Kosovo_declaration_of_independence" title="2008 Kosovo declaration of independence">unilaterally declared independence</a> on 17 February 2008. <a href="/wiki/Kosovo%E2%80%93Serbia_relations" title="Kosovo–Serbia relations">Serbia continues to claim</a> it as part of its <a href="/wiki/Autonomous_Province_of_Kosovo_and_Metohija" title="Autonomous Province of Kosovo and Metohija">own sovereign territory</a>. The two governments <a href="/wiki/Belgrade%E2%80%93Pristina_negotiations" title="Belgrade–Pristina negotiations">began to normalise relations</a> in 2013, as part of the <a href="/wiki/Brussels_Agreement_(2013)" title="Brussels Agreement (2013)">2013 Brussels Agreement</a>. Kosovo is currently recognized as an independent state by <a href="/wiki/International_recognition_of_Kosovo" title="International recognition of Kosovo">98</a> out of the 193 <a href="/wiki/Member_states_of_the_United_Nations" title="Member states of the United Nations">United Nations member states</a>. In total, <a href="/wiki/International_recognition_of_Kosovo" title="International recognition of Kosovo">113</a> UN member states recognized Kosovo at some point, of which <a href="/wiki/International_recognition_of_Kosovo" title="International recognition of Kosovo">15</a> later withdrew their recognition.</span> </li> 
        </ol>
