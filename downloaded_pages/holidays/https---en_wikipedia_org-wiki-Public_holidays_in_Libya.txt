      <p>This is a list of public <a href="/wiki/Holiday" title="Holiday">holidays</a> in <a href="/wiki/Libya" title="Libya">Libya</a>. </p> 
      <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Public_holidays">Public holidays</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Public_holidays_in_Libya&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Public holidays">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
      <table class="wikitable"> 
       <tbody>
        <tr> 
         <th>Date</th> 
         <th>English name</th> 
         <th>Remarks </th>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>17 February</td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/Revolution_Day" title="Revolution Day">February 17th Revolution</a></td> 
         <td>The <a href="/wiki/First_Battle_of_Benghazi" title="First Battle of Benghazi">start</a> of the <a href="/wiki/2011_Libyan_Revolution" class="mw-redirect" title="2011 Libyan Revolution">Libyan Revolution</a> on 17 February 2011. </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>1 May</td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/Labour_Day" title="Labour Day">Labour Day</a></td> 
         <td>Celebrates the achievements of workers. </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>16 September</td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/Martyrs%27_Day#Libya" title="Martyrs' Day">Martyrs' Day</a></td> 
         <td>Remembers Libyans killed or exiled under <a href="/wiki/History_of_Libya_as_Italian_Colony" class="mw-redirect" title="History of Libya as Italian Colony">Italian rule</a> and those who are killed in the 17 of February revolution. </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>23 October</td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/Liberation_Day" title="Liberation Day">Liberation Day</a></td> 
         <td>Day of Liberation from <a href="/wiki/History_of_Libya_under_Muammar_Gaddafi" title="History of Libya under Muammar Gaddafi">Gaddafi's Jamahiriya</a> declared on 23 October 2011. </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>24 December</td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/List_of_national_independence_days" title="List of national independence days">Independence Day</a></td> 
         <td>Anniversary of <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Libya" title="Kingdom of Libya">Libyan independence</a> in 1951. </td>
        </tr>
       </tbody>
      </table>

         <td>عيد الأضحى</td>
