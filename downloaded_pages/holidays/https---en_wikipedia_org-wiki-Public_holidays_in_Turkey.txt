      <p>The official <a href="/wiki/Holiday" title="Holiday">holidays</a> in <a href="/wiki/Turkey" title="Turkey">Turkey</a> are established by the Act 2429 of March 19, 1981 that replaced the Act 2739 of May 27, 1935. These holidays can be grouped in national and religious holidays. There are currently 14.5 days of public holiday<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> in Turkey, one of the highest number worldwide. </p>
