      <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Other_important_dates">Other important dates</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Public_holidays_in_Iraq&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Other important dates">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
      <p>These are working days at the KRG Council of Ministers, and businesses are open. Special events take place around the <a href="/wiki/Iraqi_Kurdistan" title="Iraqi Kurdistan">Iraqi Kurdistan</a> Region to mark these dates.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> </p> 
      <ul>
       <li>10 February: Kurdish Authors Union Day</li> 
       <li>18 February: Kurdish Students Union Day</li> 
       <li>1 March: Commemoration of Mustafa Barzani’s Death</li> 
       <li>7 March: Liberation of <a href="/wiki/Sulaymaniyah" title="Sulaymaniyah">Slemani City</a></li> 
       <li>8 March: Women's Day</li> 
       <li>10 March: Kurdish Clothes Day (<a href="/wiki/Iraqi_Kurdistan" title="Iraqi Kurdistan">Iraqi Kurdistan</a> only)</li> 
       <li>13 March: Liberation of <a href="/wiki/Dohuk" class="mw-redirect" title="Dohuk">Duhok City</a></li> 
       <li>16 March: <a href="/wiki/Halabja" title="Halabja">Halabja</a> Day</li> 
       <li>20 March: Liberation of <a href="/wiki/Kirkuk" title="Kirkuk">Kirkuk City</a></li> 
       <li>1 April: Assyrian New Year</li> 
       <li>14 April: Commemoration of Anfal genocide against the Kurds</li> 
       <li>16 April: Remembrance of Chemical Attack on Balisan and Sheikh Wasan</li> 
       <li>25 April: Anniversary of First Cabinet of Kurdish Government (1993)</li> 
       <li>13 June: <a href="/wiki/Sulaymaniyah" title="Sulaymaniyah">Suleimaniah City</a> Fallen and Martyrs Day</li> 
       <li>11 December: Establishment of Kurdish Women’s Union</li>
      </ul>
