       Nationally recognized holidays in Pakistan

      <p> <b>Pakistan holidays</b> are celebrated according to the Islamic or Gregorian calendars for religious and civil purposes, respectively. Religious festivals like Eid are celebrated according to the <a href="/wiki/Islamic_calendar" title="Islamic calendar">Islamic calendar</a> whereas other national holidays like international labour day, Pakistan day, and Quaid-i-Azam Day are celebrated according to the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a>. </p>
