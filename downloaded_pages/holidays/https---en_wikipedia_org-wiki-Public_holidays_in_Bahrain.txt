      <p>The following table indicates declared <a href="/wiki/Bahrain" title="Bahrain">Bahrain</a> government national <a href="/wiki/Holiday" title="Holiday">holidays</a> for the year 2015 only—cultural variants also provide opportunity for holidays tied to local events. In total there are 10 public holidays. </p>
