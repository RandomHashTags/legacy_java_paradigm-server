      <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Commemorative_Days">Commemorative Days</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Public_holidays_in_Lithuania&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Commemorative Days">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
      <p>The list of other observances (atmintinos dienos) is set by law and includes a total of 71 days,<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> not including the public holidays above.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> </p>
