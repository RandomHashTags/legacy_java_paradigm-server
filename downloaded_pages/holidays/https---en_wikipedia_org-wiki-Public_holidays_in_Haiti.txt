      <p> The following are public <a href="/wiki/Holiday" title="Holiday">holidays</a> in <a href="/wiki/Haiti" title="Haiti">Haiti</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> Many <a href="/wiki/Haitian_Vodou" title="Haitian Vodou">Vodou</a> holidays are also celebrated, but are not considered public holidays. </p>
      <p>The two most important holidays holidays for Haitian Americans are <a href="/w/index.php?title=Haitian_Independence_Day&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Haitian Independence Day (page does not exist)">Haitian Independence Day</a> and <a href="/wiki/Haitian_Flag_Day" title="Haitian Flag Day">Haitian Flag Day</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> </p> 
      <table class="wikitable"> 
       <tbody>
        <tr> 
         <th>Date</th> 
         <th>English name</th> 
         <th>Local name (in <a href="/wiki/French_language" title="French language">French</a>)</th> 
         <th>Remarks </th>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>1 January</td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/New_Year%27s_Day" title="New Year's Day">New Year's Day</a> <i>and</i> <a href="/wiki/Independence_Day" class="mw-redirect" title="Independence Day">Independence Day</a></td> 
         <td><i>Nouvel an / Jour de l'an / Premier de l'a </i>et<i> Jour de l'Indépendance</i></td> 
         <td>Commemorates the day in 1804 when <a href="/wiki/Jean-Jacques_Dessalines" title="Jean-Jacques Dessalines">Jean-Jacques Dessalines</a> declared independence and restored the country's native name. </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>2 January</td> 
         <td>Ancestry Day</td> 
         <td><i>Jour des Aieux</i></td> 
         <td>Commemorates <a href="/wiki/Ancestor" title="Ancestor">ancestors</a> and other loved ones who have died fighting for freedom. </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>6 January</td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/Epiphany_(holiday)" title="Epiphany (holiday)">Epiphany</a></td> 
         <td><i>Le Jour des Rois</i></td> 
         <td>Celebrates the Three Wise Men's visit to see the newborn Christ. </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>12 January</td> 
         <td>Remembrance Day</td> 
         <td><i>Jour De Remembrance</i></td> 
         <td>Celebrates the victims of the <a href="/wiki/2010_Haiti_earthquake" title="2010 Haiti preEarthquake">January 2010 preEarthquakes</a>. </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td><i><a href="/wiki/Moveable_feast" title="Moveable feast">moveable</a></i></td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/Haitian_Carnival" title="Haitian Carnival">Carnival</a>/<a href="/wiki/Mardi_Gras" title="Mardi Gras">Mardi Gras</a></td> 
         <td><i>Carnaval/Mardi Gras</i></td> 
         <td> </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>1 May</td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/Labour_Day" title="Labour Day">Labour and Agriculture Day</a></td> 
         <td><i>Fête du Travail / Fête des Travailleurs</i></td> 
         <td>International holiday </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>18 May</td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/Haitian_Flag_Day" title="Haitian Flag Day">Haitian Flag Day</a> and Universities Day</td> 
         <td><i>Jour du Drapeau et de l'Université</i></td> 
         <td>Celebrates its educational system and commemorates the creation of the <a href="/wiki/Flag_of_Haiti" title="Flag of Haiti">flag</a> at the 1803 Arcahaye Conference. </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>15 August</td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/Assumption_of_Mary" title="Assumption of Mary">Assumption of Mary</a></td> 
         <td><i>L'Assomption de Marie</i></td> 
         <td>Roman Catholic holiday </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>17 October</td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/Jean-Jacques_Dessalines" title="Jean-Jacques Dessalines">Dessalines Day</a><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></td> 
         <td><i>Anniversaire de la mort de Dessalines</i></td> 
         <td>Commemorates the death of <a href="/wiki/Jean-Jacques_Dessalines" title="Jean-Jacques Dessalines">Jean-Jacques Dessalines</a>. </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>1 November</td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/All_Saints_Day" class="mw-redirect" title="All Saints Day">All Saints Day</a></td> 
         <td><i>La Toussaint</i></td> 
         <td>Roman Catholic holiday; commemorates the <a href="/wiki/Saint" title="Saint">sainthood</a>. </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>2 November</td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/All_Souls%27_Day" title="All Souls' Day">All Souls' Day</a></td> 
         <td><i>Jour des Morts</i></td> 
         <td>Roman Catholic holiday; commemorates the faithful departed. </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>18 November</td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Verti%C3%A8res" title="Battle of Vertières">Battle of Vertières Day</a></td> 
         <td><i>Vertières</i></td> 
         <td>Commemorates the victory over the French in the Battle of Vertières in the year 1803.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>5 December</td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/Discovery_Day" title="Discovery Day">Discovery Day</a></td> 
         <td><i>Découverte d'Haïti</i></td> 
         <td>Commemorates <a href="/wiki/Christopher_Columbus" title="Christopher Columbus">Christopher Columbus</a>' landing on <a href="/wiki/Hispaniola" title="Hispaniola">Hispaniola</a> in 1492. </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>25 December</td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/Christmas" title="Christmas">Christmas</a></td> 
         <td><i>Noël</i></td> 
         <td>Celebrates the <a href="/wiki/Nativity_of_Jesus" title="Nativity of Jesus">nativity of Jesus</a>. </td>
        </tr>

      </table>
