      <p>This is the <b>list of public holidays in <a href="/wiki/Kyrgyzstan" title="Kyrgyzstan">Kyrgyzstan</a></b>: </p> 
      <table class="wikitable" style="font-size:95%;"> 
       <tbody>

        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>January 1</td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/New_Year%27s_Day" title="New Year's Day">New Year's Day</a> </td> 
         <td>Жаңы жыл </td> 
         <td style="font-size:95%;"> </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>January 7</td> 
         <td>Eastern Orthodox <a href="/wiki/Christmas" title="Christmas">Christmas</a> </td> 
         <td>Иса пайгамбардын туулган күнү </td> 
         <td style="font-size:95%;">Celebrated by local Orthodox Christians </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>February 23</td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/Red_Army_Day" class="mw-redirect" title="Red Army Day">Fatherland Defender's Day</a> </td> 
         <td>Мекенди коргоочунун күнү </td> 
         <td style="font-size:95%;">Formation of the Red Army in February 1918. </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>March 8</td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/International_Women%27s_Day" title="International Women's Day">International Women's Day</a> </td> 
         <td style="white-space:nowrap;">Эл аралык аялдар күнү </td> 
         <td style="font-size:95%;"> </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>March 21</td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/Nowruz" title="Nowruz">Nooruz Mairamy</a> </td> 
         <td>Нооруз </td> 
         <td style="font-size:95%;">Which is originally the <a href="/wiki/Nowruz" title="Nowruz">Persian New Year</a>, is traditionally a springtime holiday marking the beginning of a new year. </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>April 7</td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/Day_of_the_People%27s_April_Revolution" title="Day of the People's April Revolution">Day of the People's April Revolution</a><sup id="cite_ref-:0_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-1">[1]</a></sup> </td> 
         <td style="white-space:nowrap;">Элдик Апрель революциясы күнү </td> 
         <td style="font-size:95%;">Commemorates the <a href="/wiki/Kyrgyz_Revolution_of_2010" title="Kyrgyz Revolution of 2010">Second Kyrgyz Revolution</a> of 2010. </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>May 1</td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/International_Workers%27_Day" title="International Workers' Day">International Workers' Day</a> </td> 
         <td>Эмгек күнү </td> 
         <td style="font-size:95%;"> </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>May 5</td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/Constitution_Day" title="Constitution Day">Constitution Day</a> </td> 
         <td>Кыргыз Республикасынын конституция күнү </td> 
         <td style="font-size:95%;"> </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>May 9</td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/Victory_Day_(Eastern_Europe)" class="mw-redirect" title="Victory Day (Eastern Europe)">Great Patriotic War Against Fascism Victory Day</a> </td> 
         <td>Жеңиш күнү </td> 
         <td style="font-size:95%;">A holiday in the former Soviet Union carried over <p>to present-day Kazakhstan and other former republics (Except Baltic Countries and Ukraine). </p> </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>August 31</td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/Independence_Day_(Kyrgyzstan)" title="Independence Day (Kyrgyzstan)">Independence Day</a> </td> 
         <td>Кыргыз Республикасынын Эгемендүүлүк күнү </td> 
         <td style="font-size:95%;"> </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>November 7–8</td> 
         <td><a href="/w/index.php?title=Days_of_History_and_Commemoration_of_Ancestors&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Days of History and Commemoration of Ancestors (page does not exist)">Days of History and Commemoration of Ancestors</a> </td> 
         <td>Тарых күн жана аталардын эскерүү </td> 
         <td style="font-size:95%;">Two-day holiday commemorating the national liberation uprising and the tragic events of 1916 known as <a href="/wiki/Urkun" class="mw-redirect" title="Urkun">Urkun</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-ТК_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ТК-3">[3]</a></sup> 
          <hr> <p>Previously, from independence to 2017, the country celebrated the <a href="/wiki/October_Revolution" title="October Revolution">Great October Socialist Revolution</a>. It has now been replaced with День примирения и согласия ("Day of Reconciliation and Agreement"), celebrated on a Nov. 7 (at least officially) before amendments in Labour Codex (adopted in December 2004, new holiday, which celebrates at November 4 is the People Unity Day ("День народного единства)" </p> </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>Defined by lunar calendar</td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/Eid_al-Fitr" title="Eid al-Fitr">Orozo Ait</a> </td> 
         <td>Орозо айт </td> 
         <td style="font-size:95%;">Feast of Fast-breaking </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td>Celebrated 70 days after the Orozo Ait</td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/Eid_al-Adha" title="Eid al-Adha">Kurman Ait</a> </td> 
         <td>Курман айт </td> 
         <td style="font-size:95%;">Feast of Sacrifice </td>
        </tr>
       </tbody>
      </table> 
      <p>Two additional Muslim holidays <i>Orozo Ait</i> and <i>Kurman Ait</i> are defined by lunar calendar. </p>
