      <p>This is a list of <b><a href="/wiki/Public_holidays" class="mw-redirect" title="Public holidays">public holidays</a></b> in <b><a href="/wiki/Montenegro" title="Montenegro">Montenegro</a>. </b> </p> 
      <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Official_holidays">Official holidays</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Public_holidays_in_Montenegro&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Official holidays">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
      <p>All state and other holidays are non-working days. The religious holidays are non-working days for the specific religious communities. </p>
      <p><i>Note:</i> If the first day of State and Other holidays falls on Sunday, then the next two working days are non-working days. And, If the second day of State and Other holidays falls on Sunday, then the next working day is a non-working day. </p>
