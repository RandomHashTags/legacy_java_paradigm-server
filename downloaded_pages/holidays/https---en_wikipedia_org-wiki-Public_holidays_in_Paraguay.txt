      <p>All holidays in Paraguay can be moved by presidential decree, but there has to be a presidential decree for such a change. The following holidays are the exception (dates are fixed and to not change): January 1, May 14, December 8, December 25, Maundy Thursday and Good Friday.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> </p>
