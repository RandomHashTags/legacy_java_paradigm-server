      <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Ambazonian_holidays">Ambazonian holidays</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Public_holidays_in_Cameroon&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Ambazonian holidays">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
