      <p>The following are <b>public <a href="/wiki/Holiday" title="Holiday">holidays</a> (የኢትዮጵያ:ብሔራዊ:በዓል) in <a href="/wiki/Ethiopia" title="Ethiopia">Ethiopia</a></b>. Many holidays follow the <a href="/wiki/Ethiopian_Orthodox_Tewahedo_Church" title="Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Church">Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Church</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> </p>
