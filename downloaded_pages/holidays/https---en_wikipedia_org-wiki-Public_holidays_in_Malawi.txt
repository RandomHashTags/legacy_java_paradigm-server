      <p>This is a list of <a href="/wiki/Public_holidays" class="mw-redirect" title="Public holidays">public holidays</a> in <a href="/wiki/Malawi" title="Malawi">Malawi</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> </p>
      <p>Where the relevant event falls on a non-working day, the holiday is on the next working day. </p>

         <td>1 January </td> 
         <td> </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/John_Chilembwe" title="John Chilembwe">John Chilembwe Day</a><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> </td> 
         <td>15 January </td> 
         <td>Honors the Reverend Chilembwe who led the first nationalist uprising in 1914-1915 protesting against forced labor and African involvement in World War I.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/Martyrs%27_Day#Malawi" title="Martyrs' Day">Martyrs' Day</a> Holiday </td> 
         <td>3 March (alternate 4 March) </td> 
         <td>Honors the political heroes who gave their lives in the struggle against British colonialism.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/Good_Friday" title="Good Friday">Good Friday</a> </td>

         <td>1 May (alternate 2 or 3 May) </td> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/International_Workers%27_Day" title="International Workers' Day">International Workers' Day</a> </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/Hastings_Banda" title="Hastings Banda">President Kamuzu Banda's Birthday</a> Holiday </td> 
         <td>14 May </td> 
         <td>Malawi's first president<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/Eid_al-Fitr" title="Eid al-Fitr">Eid al-Fitr</a> </td> 
         <td>1 <a href="/wiki/Shawwal" title="Shawwal">Shawwal</a> (subject to moon sighting) </td> 
         <td>Festival of Breaking the Fast </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/Independence_Day" class="mw-redirect" title="Independence Day">Independence Day</a> Holiday </td> 
         <td>6 July </td> 
         <td>Marks Malawi's independence from the United Kingdom in 1966.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> </td>
        </tr> 
        <tr> 
         <td><a href="/wiki/Mother%27s_Day" title="Mother's Day">Mother's Day</a> Holiday </td>
