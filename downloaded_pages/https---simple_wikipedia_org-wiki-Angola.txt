      <p><b>Angola</b>, (<span class="rt-commentedText nowrap"><span class="IPA nopopups noexcerpt"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA/English" title="Help:IPA/English">/<span style="border-bottom:1px dotted"><span title="/√¶/: 'a' in 'bad'">√¶</span><span title="'n' in 'nigh'">n</span><span title="/Àà/: primary stress follows">Àà</span><span title="/…°/: 'g' in 'guy'">…°</span><span title="/o ä/: 'o' in 'code'">o ä</span><span title="'l' in 'lie'">l</span><span title="/…ô/: 'a' in 'about'">…ô</span></span>/</a></span></span>), officially the <b>Republic of Angola</b>, is a country in southern Africa bordered by <a href="/wiki/Namibia" title="Namibia">Namibia</a> on the south, the <a href="/wiki/Democratic_Republic_of_the_Congo" title="Democratic Republic of the Congo">Democratic Republic of the Congo</a> on the north, and <a href="/wiki/Zambia" title="Zambia">Zambia</a> on the east; its west coast is on the Atlantic Ocean with <a href="/wiki/Luanda" title="Luanda">Luanda</a> as its capital city. </p>
      <p>Angola is a member state of the <a href="/wiki/African_Union" title="African Union">African Union</a>, the <a href="/w/index.php?title=Community_of_Portuguese_Language_Countries&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Community of Portuguese Language Countries (not yet started)">Community of Portuguese Language Countries</a>, the <a href="/w/index.php?title=Latin_Union&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Latin Union (not yet started)">Latin Union</a>, <a href="/wiki/South_Atlantic_Peace_and_Cooperation_Zone" title="South Atlantic Peace and Cooperation Zone">South Atlantic Peace and Cooperation Zone</a> and the <a href="/w/index.php?title=Southern_African_Development_Community&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Southern African Development Community (not yet started)">Southern African Development Community</a>. </p>

      <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Angola&amp;veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Change section: History">change</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Angola&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Change section: History">change source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
      <p>Portugal built up its power in Angola from the late 15th to the middle 20th century. </p>
      <p>After independence there was a <a href="/wiki/Angolan_Civil_War" title="Angolan Civil War">civil war</a> from 1975 to 2002. Cuba and the <a href="/wiki/Soviet_Bloc" class="mw-redirect" title="Soviet Bloc">Soviet Bloc</a> supported the ruling People's Movement for the Liberation of Angola (MPLA). South Africa supported the insurgent anti-communist National Union for the Total Independence of Angola (UNITA). The war ended after the rebel leader was killed. </p> 
      <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Angola&amp;veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Change section: Geography">change</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Angola&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Change section: Geography">change source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
      <p>Angola is the world's twenty-third largest country. Angola is bordered by <a href="/wiki/Namibia" title="Namibia">Namibia</a> to the south, <a href="/wiki/Zambia" title="Zambia">Zambia</a> to the east, the <a href="/wiki/Democratic_Republic_of_the_Congo" title="Democratic Republic of the Congo">Democratic Republic of the Congo</a> to the north-east, and the South <a href="/wiki/Atlantic_Ocean" title="Atlantic Ocean">Atlantic Ocean</a> to the west. </p> 
      <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Climate">Climate</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Angola&amp;veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Change section: Climate">change</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Angola&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Change section: Climate">change source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
      <p>Angola's average temperature on the coast is 16&nbsp;¬∞C (61&nbsp;¬∞F) in the winter and 21&nbsp;¬∞C (70&nbsp;¬∞F) in the summer. It has two seasons; dry (May to October) and hot rainy (November to April). </p>
