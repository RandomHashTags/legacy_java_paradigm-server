      <p>This is a list representing <b><a href="/wiki/Time_zone" title="Time zone">time zones</a> by country</b>. Countries are ranked by total number of time zones on their territory. Time zones of a country include that of <a href="/wiki/Dependent_territory" title="Dependent territory">dependent territories</a> (except <a href="/wiki/Territorial_claims_in_Antarctica" title="Territorial claims in Antarctica">Antarctic claims</a>). <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">France</a>, including its overseas territories, has the most time zones with 12. Many countries have <a href="/wiki/Daylight_saving_time" title="Daylight saving time">daylight saving time</a>, one added hour during the local summer, but this list does not include that information. The <a href="/wiki/Coordinated_Universal_Time" title="Coordinated Universal Time">UTC</a> offset in the list is not valid in practice during daylight saving time. </p>
