      <p><b>Micronesia</b>, officially the <b>Federated States of Micronesia</b>, is an <a href="/wiki/Island" title="Island">island</a> <a href="/wiki/Nation" title="Nation">nation</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Pacific_Ocean" title="Pacific Ocean">Pacific Ocean</a>, northeast of <a href="/wiki/Papua_New_Guinea" title="Papua New Guinea">Papua New Guinea</a>. The country is a <a href="/wiki/Sovereign_state" title="Sovereign state">sovereign state</a> in <a href="/w/index.php?title=Associated_state&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Associated state (not yet started)">free association</a> with the <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a>. The <a href="/wiki/Capital_city" title="Capital city">capital city</a> of Federated States of Micronesia is <a href="/wiki/Palikir" title="Palikir">Palikir</a>. </p>
      <p>The Federated States of Micronesia were formerly part of the <a href="/w/index.php?title=Trust_Territory_of_the_Pacific_Islands&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Trust Territory of the Pacific Islands (not yet started)">Trust Territory of the Pacific Islands</a>, a <a href="/wiki/United_Nations" title="United Nations">United Nations</a> Trust Territory under US administration. In 1979 they adopted a constitution, and in 1986 independence was attained under a <a href="/w/index.php?title=Compact_of_Free_Association&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Compact of Free Association (not yet started)">Compact of Free Association</a> with the United States. Present concerns include large-scale unemployment, overfishing, and overdependence on U.S. aid. </p>
      <p>The Federated States of Micronesia is hundreds of small islands divided in seven territories in <a href="/wiki/Micronesia" title="Micronesia">Micronesia</a>. The term <i>Micronesia</i> may mean the Federated States or to the region as a whole. This area is in Oceania. </p>
