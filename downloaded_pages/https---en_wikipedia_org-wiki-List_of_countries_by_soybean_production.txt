      <p>This is a <b>list of countries by <a href="/wiki/Soybean" title="Soybean">soybean</a> production</b> in 2018, based on data from the <a href="/wiki/Food_and_Agriculture_Organization_Corporate_Statistical_Database" title="Food and Agriculture Organization Corporate Statistical Database">Food and Agriculture Organization Corporate Statistical Database</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The total world production for soybeans in 2018 was 348,712,311 <a href="/wiki/Tonne" title="Tonne">metric tonnes</a>. Brazil was the largest producer, accounting for 36% of world production.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> </p>
