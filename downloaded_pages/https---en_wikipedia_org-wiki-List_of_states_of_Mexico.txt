      <p>The <b>states of Mexico</b> are first-level administrative territorial entities of the country of Mexico, which officially is named <a href="/wiki/Mexico" title="Mexico">United Mexican States</a>. There are 32 federal entities in Mexico (31 states and the capital, <a href="/wiki/Mexico_City" title="Mexico City">Mexico City</a>, as a separate entity without being formally a state).<sup id="cite_ref-guardianname_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-guardianname-1">[1]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> </p>
      <p>The states are further divided into <a href="/wiki/Municipalities_of_Mexico" title="Municipalities of Mexico">municipalities</a>. In Mexico City the municipalities are known as <i>alcaldías</i> (similar to other state's municipalities but with different administrative powers).<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup><sup class="noprint Inline-Template noprint Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Verifiability#Wikipedia_and_sources_that_mirror_or_use_it" title="Wikipedia:Verifiability"><span title="This claim cites another Wikipedia article. Articles need references to reliable third-party sources. (June 2020)">circular reference</span></a></i>]</sup> </p>

      <p><b>Notes:</b> </p> 
      <ol>
       <li>Joined the federation with the name of <a href="/wiki/Coahuila_y_Tejas" title="Coahuila y Tejas">Coahuila y Tejas</a>.</li> 
       <li>Joined the federation with the name of <a href="/wiki/Estado_de_Occidente" title="Estado de Occidente">Estado de Occidente</a> also recognized as <a href="/wiki/Sonora_y_Sinaloa" class="mw-redirect" title="Sonora y Sinaloa">Sonora y Sinaloa</a>.</li> 
       <li>Joined the federation as <a href="/wiki/Republic_of_Yucatan#First_Republic_of_Yucatán_and_the_federal_pact_with_Mexico" class="mw-redirect" title="Republic of Yucatan">República Federada de Yucatán</a><sup id="cite_ref-22" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-22">[22]</a></sup> (English: <span lang="en">Federated Republic of Yucatán</span>) formed by the current states of Yucatán, Campeche and Quintana Roo. Became independent in 1841 constituting the second <a href="/wiki/Republic_of_Yucat%C3%A1n" title="Republic of Yucatán">Republic of Yucatán</a> and definitively rejoined in 1848.</li> 
       <li>States of <a href="/wiki/Nuevo_Le%C3%B3n" title="Nuevo León">Nuevo León</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tamaulipas" title="Tamaulipas">Tamaulipas</a> and <a href="/wiki/Coahuila" title="Coahuila">Coahuila</a> became independent <i><a href="/wiki/De_facto" title="De facto">de facto</a></i> in 1840 to form the <a href="/wiki/Rep%C3%BAblica_del_R%C3%ADo_Grande" class="mw-redirect" title="República del Río Grande">República del Río Grande</a> (English: <span lang="en">Republic of the Rio Grande</span>); never consolidated its independence because independent forces were defeated by the centralist forces.<sup id="cite_ref-23" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-23">[23]</a></sup></li> 
       <li>The State of <a href="/wiki/Tabasco" title="Tabasco">Tabasco</a> seceded from Mexico on two occasions, the first on February 13, 1841, rejoining again on December 2, 1842. And the second time was from November 9, 1846 to December 9, 1846</li> 
       <li>Inc the remote <a href="/wiki/Revillagigedo_Islands" title="Revillagigedo Islands">Revillagigedo Islands</a>, which are federally administered.</li>
      </ol>
