      <p><b>Uruguay</b> (officially <b>Oriental Republic of Uruguay</b>) is a country in <a href="/wiki/South_America" title="South America">South America</a>. The language spoken there is Spanish. Its <a href="/wiki/Capital_(city)" class="mw-redirect" title="Capital (city)">capital</a> and largest <a href="/wiki/City" title="City">city</a> is <a href="/wiki/Montevideo" title="Montevideo">Montevideo</a>. Uruguay is bordered by two large neighbors, <a href="/wiki/Brazil" title="Brazil">Brazil</a> and <a href="/wiki/Argentina" title="Argentina">Argentina</a>. The only country in South America that is smaller than Uruguay is <a href="/wiki/Suriname" title="Suriname">Suriname</a>. The land is mostly flat, and there are many farms in the countryside. </p>
      <p>The area around Uruguay was fought over by the Portuguese and <a href="/wiki/Spanish_Empire" title="Spanish Empire">Spanish Empire</a>s in the 18th century. In the 1810s it was the center of a Federal League of states in the <a href="/wiki/R%C3%ADo_de_la_Plata" title="Río de la Plata">Río de la Plata</a> region. Argentina and Brazil took the majority in wars, leaving Uruguay as independent. </p>
