      <p>This is a <b>list of countries by <a href="/wiki/Pineapple" title="Pineapple">pineapple</a> production</b> in 2016 and 2017, based on data from the <a href="/wiki/Food_and_Agriculture_Organization_Corporate_Statistical_Database" title="Food and Agriculture Organization Corporate Statistical Database">Food and Agriculture Organization Corporate Statistical Database</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The estimated total world production for pineapples in 2017 was 27,402,956 <a href="/wiki/Tonne" title="Tonne">metric tonnes</a>, an increase of 4.6% from 26,195,845 tonnes in 2016.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[n 1]</a></sup> Dependent territories are shown in italics. </p>
