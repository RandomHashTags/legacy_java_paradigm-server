      <p>This is a <b>list of countries by <a href="/wiki/Potato" title="Potato">potato</a> production</b> in 2016, based on data from the <a href="/wiki/Food_and_Agriculture_Organization_Corporate_Statistical_Database" title="Food and Agriculture Organization Corporate Statistical Database">Food and Agriculture Organization Corporate Statistical Database</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The total world production for potatoes in 2016 was 376,826,967 <a href="/wiki/Tonne" title="Tonne">metric tonnes</a>. China was by far the largest producer, accounting for 26.3% of world production. Dependent territories are shown in italics. Total production in 2019 was 423 mln tonnes. <sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> </p>
