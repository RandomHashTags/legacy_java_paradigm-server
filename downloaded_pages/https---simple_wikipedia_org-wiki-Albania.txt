It has a <a href="/wiki/Coast" title="Coast">coast</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Adriatic_Sea" title="Adriatic Sea">Adriatic Sea</a> in the west, and a coast on the <a href="/wiki/Ionian_Sea" title="Ionian Sea">Ionian Sea</a> in the southwest. The <a href="/wiki/Official_language" title="Official language">official language</a> is <a href="/wiki/Albanian_language" title="Albanian language">Albanian</a>. The <a href="/wiki/National_anthem" title="National anthem">national anthem</a> is <i><a href="/wiki/Hymni_i_Flamurit" class="mw-redirect" title="Hymni i Flamurit">Hymni i Flamurit</a></i>. </p>

      <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Albania&amp;veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Change section: History">change</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Albania&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Change section: History">change source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
      <p>Albania was the southern part of <i>Illyria</i> during the <a href="/wiki/Roman_Empire" title="Roman Empire">Roman Empire</a>. In the Middle Ages the Albanians were ruled by many foreign countries, including the <a href="/wiki/Byzantine_Empire" title="Byzantine Empire">Byzantine</a> and <a href="/wiki/Venice" title="Venice">Venetian</a> Empires and during the 16th century resistance to the <a href="/wiki/Ottoman_Empire" title="Ottoman Empire">Ottoman Empire</a>'s rule was led by the Albanian national hero <i>Skanderberg</i> but his real name was Gjergj Kastrioti. After four centuries of Turkish domination, finally in the 20th century an independent Albania was created. In 1939 Albania was conquered by fascist <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italy</a> and was part of the Kingdom of Italy until 1943. After <a href="/wiki/World_War_II" title="World War II">World War II</a> Albania became independent again, under <a href="/wiki/Communism" title="Communism">communist</a> rule. It was ruled by <a href="/wiki/Enver_Hoxha" title="Enver Hoxha">Enver Hoxha</a>, who died in <a href="/wiki/1985" title="1985">1985</a>. <a href="/wiki/Ramiz_Alia" title="Ramiz Alia">Ramiz Alia</a> took over and also later became President when, in 1992, Albania returned to democracy, and it now has friendly relations with the <a href="/wiki/European_Union" title="European Union">European Union</a>, which it has applied to join. </p>

      <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Armed_forces">Armed forces</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Albania&amp;veaction=edit&amp;section=4" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Change section: Armed forces">change</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Albania&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Change section: Armed forces">change source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
      <p>The <a href="/wiki/Military_of_Albania" class="mw-redirect" title="Military of Albania">Albanian Armed Forces</a> were first formed after independence in 1912. Albania reduced the number of active troops from 65,000 in 1988<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> to 14,500 in 2009.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> The military now is mostly a small fleet of aircraft and sea vessels. </p> 
      <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Albania&amp;veaction=edit&amp;section=5" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Change section: Geography">change</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Albania&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Change section: Geography">change source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
      <p>Albania has a total area of 28,748 square kilometers. Albania's coastline length is 476&nbsp;km (296&nbsp;mi).<sup id="cite_ref-Albania_pdf_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Albania_pdf-9">[9]</a></sup><sup class="reference" style="white-space:nowrap;">:240</sup> It goes along the <a href="/wiki/Adriatic_Sea" title="Adriatic Sea">Adriatic</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ionian_Sea" title="Ionian Sea">Ionian Seas</a>. On the coast there are mild, wet winters and warm, sunny, and rather dry summers. </p>
      <p>70% of the country is mountains. The highest mountain is <a href="/wiki/Mount_Korab" title="Mount Korab">Korab</a>. </p>
      <p>Albania is bordered by <a href="/wiki/Greece" title="Greece">Greece</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Republic_of_Macedonia" class="mw-redirect" title="Republic of Macedonia">Republic of North Macedonia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Montenegro" title="Montenegro">Montenegro</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kosovo" title="Kosovo">Kosovo</a>. A short stretch of <a href="/wiki/Sea" title="Sea">sea</a> separates Albania from the far southeast of <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italy</a>. </p> 
      <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Flora_and_fauna">Flora and fauna</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Albania&amp;veaction=edit&amp;section=6" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Change section: Flora and fauna">change</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Albania&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Change section: Flora and fauna">change source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>

      <p>Although a small country, Albania has a lot of variety in plants and animals. The total number of plants is over 3250 species. There are over 350 bird species, 330 freshwater and marine fish and 80 mammal species. The <a href="/wiki/Golden_Eagle" class="mw-redirect" title="Golden Eagle">Golden Eagle</a> is the national symbol of Albania. </p>
