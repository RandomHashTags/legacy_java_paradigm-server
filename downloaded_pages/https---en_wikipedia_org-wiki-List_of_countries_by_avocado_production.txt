      <p>This is a <b>list of countries by <a href="/wiki/Avocado" title="Avocado">avocado</a> production</b> from the years 2016 to 2018, based on data from the <a href="/wiki/Food_and_Agriculture_Organization_Corporate_Statistical_Database" title="Food and Agriculture Organization Corporate Statistical Database">Food and Agriculture Organization Corporate Statistical Database</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The estimated total world production for avocados in 2018 was 6,407,171 <a href="/wiki/Tonne" title="Tonne">metric tonnes</a>, up 6.7% from 6,005,285 tonnes in 2017.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[n 1]</a></sup> Mexico was the largest producer, account for 34% of global production. Dependent territories are shown in italics. </p>
