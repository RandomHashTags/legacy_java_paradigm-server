      <p>The <b>Republic of the Congo</b> is a country in <a href="/wiki/Africa" title="Africa">Africa</a>. Its <a href="/wiki/Capital_city" title="Capital city">capital city</a> is <a href="/wiki/Brazzaville" title="Brazzaville">Brazzaville</a>. The country was a former colony of <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">France</a>; it became independent on 15 August 1960. The north of the country has very large areas of <a href="/wiki/Rainforest" title="Rainforest">rainforest</a>, but in the south are many <a href="/wiki/Farm" title="Farm">farms</a> which grow cashcrops like <a href="/wiki/Banana" title="Banana">bananas</a>, <a href="/wiki/Peanut" title="Peanut">peanuts</a>. </p>

      <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Republic_of_the_Congo&amp;veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Change section: Geography">change</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Republic_of_the_Congo&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Change section: Geography">change source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
      <p>Congo is in the central-western part of <a href="/wiki/Sub-Saharan_Africa" title="Sub-Saharan Africa">sub-Saharan Africa</a>. It is along the <a href="/wiki/Equator" title="Equator">Equator</a>. To the south and east of it is the <a href="/wiki/Democratic_Republic_of_Congo" class="mw-redirect" title="Democratic Republic of Congo">Democratic Republic of Congo</a>. It is also bordered by <a href="/wiki/Gabon" title="Gabon">Gabon</a> to the west, <a href="/wiki/Cameroon" title="Cameroon">Cameroon</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Central_African_Republic" title="Central African Republic">Central African Republic</a> to the north, and <a href="/w/index.php?title=Cabinda_(province)&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Cabinda (province) (not yet started)">Cabinda</a> (<a href="/wiki/Angola" title="Angola">Angola</a>) to the southwest. It has a short <a href="/wiki/Atlantic_Ocean" title="Atlantic Ocean">Atlantic</a> coast. </p>
      <p>The capital, <a href="/wiki/Brazzaville" title="Brazzaville">Brazzaville</a>, is on the <a href="/wiki/Congo_River" title="Congo River">Congo River</a>, directly across from <a href="/wiki/Kinshasa" title="Kinshasa">Kinshasa</a>, the capital of the <a href="/wiki/Democratic_Republic_of_the_Congo" title="Democratic Republic of the Congo">Democratic Republic of the Congo</a>. </p>
