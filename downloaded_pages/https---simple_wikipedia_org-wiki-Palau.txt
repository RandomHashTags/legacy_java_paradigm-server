      <p><b>Palau</b>, officially the <b>Republic of Palau</b>, is an <a href="/wiki/Borderless_country" class="mw-redirect" title="Borderless country">island nation</a>. It is in the <a href="/wiki/Pacific_Ocean" title="Pacific Ocean">Pacific Ocean</a>. Palau is 300 <a href="/wiki/Mile" title="Mile">miles</a> (500&nbsp;<a href="/wiki/Kilometre" title="Kilometre">km</a>) east of the <a href="/wiki/Philippines" title="Philippines">Philippines</a>. It got independence from <a href="/wiki/United_Nations" title="United Nations">United Nations</a> trusteeship administration (administered by the <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States of America</a>) in 1994. It is one of the world's youngest and smallest nations. It is sometimes referred to in <a href="/wiki/English_language" title="English language">English</a> under its native name <b>Belau</b>. The <a href="/wiki/Capital_city" title="Capital city">capital city</a> of Palau is <a href="/w/index.php?title=Melekeok&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Melekeok (not yet started)">Melekeok</a>. This has been true since October 1, 2006. </p>

      <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Climate">Climate</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Palau&amp;veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Change section: Climate">change</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Palau&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Change section: Climate">change source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
      <p>Palau has a tropical <a href="/wiki/Climate" title="Climate">climate</a> all year round. Average yearly temperature is around 82&nbsp;°<a href="/wiki/Fahrenheit" title="Fahrenheit">F</a> (27&nbsp;°<a href="/wiki/Celsius" title="Celsius">C</a>). </p>
