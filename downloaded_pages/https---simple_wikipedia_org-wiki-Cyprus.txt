   <h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading" lang="en">Cyprus</h1>

      <p><i>This article is about the island. For the country, see <a href="/wiki/Republic_of_Cyprus" title="Republic of Cyprus">Republic of Cyprus</a> and <a href="/wiki/Northern_Cyprus" title="Northern Cyprus">Northern Cyprus</a>.</i> </p>

      <p><b>Cyprus</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Mediterranean_Sea" title="Mediterranean Sea">Mediterranean</a> island off the coasts of <a href="/wiki/Syria" title="Syria">Syria</a> and <a href="/wiki/Turkey" title="Turkey">Turkey</a>. It is the third-largest <a href="/wiki/Island" title="Island">island</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Mediterranean" class="mw-redirect" title="Mediterranean">Mediterranean</a>; <a href="/wiki/Sicily" title="Sicily">Sicily</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sardinia" title="Sardinia">Sardinia</a> are larger. It is slightly larger than <a href="/wiki/Puerto_Rico" title="Puerto Rico">Puerto Rico</a>. Geographically, it is part of <a href="/wiki/Asia" title="Asia">Asia</a> and in the <a href="/wiki/Middle_East" title="Middle East">Middle East</a>, but for political reasons, it is sometimes counted as being part of <a href="/wiki/Europe" title="Europe">Europe</a>. There are two states on the island: </p> 
      <ul>
       <li>The <a href="/wiki/Republic_of_Cyprus" title="Republic of Cyprus">Republic of Cyprus</a> (internationally recognized) is also part of the <a href="/wiki/European_Union" title="European Union">European Union</a>. Most of the world considers the whole island to be the Republic of Cyprus.</li> 
       <li>The independent <a href="/wiki/Turkish_Republic_of_Northern_Cyprus" class="mw-redirect" title="Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus">Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus</a> is the northern part of the island. The only nation that recognizes this as its own country is <a href="/wiki/Turkey" title="Turkey">Turkey</a>.</li>
      </ul> 
      <p>These states are separated by a buffer zone, which is controlled by the <a href="/wiki/United_Nations" title="United Nations">United Nations</a>. In addition to the two countries, there are <a href="/wiki/Akrotiri_and_Dhekelia" title="Akrotiri and Dhekelia">Akrotiri and Dhekelia</a>, two territories which are under <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> control, and which are used as military bases. </p>
      <p>In its thousands of years of recorded history, many empires have ruled Cyprus. They include <a href="/wiki/Assyria" title="Assyria">Assyria</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ancient_Egypt" title="Ancient Egypt">ancient Egypt</a> <a href="/wiki/Achaemenid_Empire" title="Achaemenid Empire">Persia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Alexander_the_Great" title="Alexander the Great">Alexander the Great</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Roman_Empire" title="Roman Empire">Roman Empire</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Byzantine" class="mw-redirect" title="Byzantine">Byzantines</a>, Arab <a href="/wiki/Caliphate" title="Caliphate">caliphates</a> for a short period, <a href="/wiki/French_colonial_empire" title="French colonial empire">France</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Republic_of_Venice" title="Republic of Venice">Republic of Venice</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Ottoman_Empire" title="Ottoman Empire">Ottoman Empire</a>. </p>
