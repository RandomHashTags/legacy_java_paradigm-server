      <p>This is a <b>list of countries by <a href="/wiki/Cucumber" title="Cucumber">cucumber</a> production</b> from the years 2016 to 2018, based on data from the <a href="/wiki/Food_and_Agriculture_Organization_Corporate_Statistical_Database" title="Food and Agriculture Organization Corporate Statistical Database">Food and Agriculture Organization Corporate Statistical Database</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[n 1]</a></sup> The estimated total world production for cucumbers in 2018 was 75,219,440 <a href="/wiki/Tonne" title="Tonne">metric tonnes</a>, down 3.4% from 77,896,545 tonnes in 2017.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">[n 2]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a> was by far the largest producer, accounting for nearly 75% of global production at 56,240,428 tonnes. Dependent territories are shown in italics. </p>
