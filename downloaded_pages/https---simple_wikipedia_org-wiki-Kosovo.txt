      <p><b>Kosova</b> is part of Serbia , located in south of Serbia. </p>
      <p>Kosovo was part of the <a href="/w/index.php?title=Dardani&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Dardani (not yet started)">Dardani</a> lands in <a href="/wiki/Ancient_history" title="Ancient history">ancient times</a>. The Dardani were <a href="/wiki/Invasion" title="Invasion">conquered</a> by the <a href="/wiki/Roman_Empire" title="Roman Empire">Roman Empire</a>. After the <a href="/wiki/Failure" title="Failure">fall</a> of Rome, it became part of the <a href="/wiki/Byzantine_Empire" title="Byzantine Empire">Byzantine Empire</a>. It was taken over by them and the <a href="/w/index.php?title=Bulgarian_Empire&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Bulgarian Empire (not yet started)">Bulgarian Empire</a>. The <a href="/w/index.php?title=Servian_Empire&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Servian Empire (not yet started)">Servian Empire</a> also defeated then. Soon after the Servian defeat in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Kosovo" title="Battle of Kosovo">Battle of Kosovo</a>, it became part of the <a href="/wiki/Ottoman_Empire" title="Ottoman Empire">Ottoman Empire</a>. </p>
      <p>When the <a href="/wiki/Turkic_peoples" title="Turkic peoples">Turks</a> left the <a href="/wiki/Balkans" title="Balkans">Balkans</a>, it became part of the <a href="/w/index.php?title=Kingdom_of_Serbia&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Kingdom of Serbia (not yet started)">Kingdom of Serbia</a>. In <a href="/wiki/World_War_I" title="World War I">World War I</a>, it briefly became a part of the <a href="/wiki/Austro-Hungarian_Empire" class="mw-redirect" title="Austro-Hungarian Empire">Austro-Hungarian Empire</a>. Then in <a href="/wiki/World_War_II" title="World War II">World War II</a>, after <a href="/wiki/Nazi_Germany" title="Nazi Germany">Germany</a>, <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italy</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Bulgaria" title="Bulgaria">Bulgaria</a> <a href="/wiki/Invasion" title="Invasion">invaded</a>, it was taken by the <a href="/w/index.php?title=Italian_Empire&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Italian Empire (not yet started)">Italian Empire</a><a href="/wiki/Albania" title="Albania">Kingdom of Albania</a>. After the war, it became part of <a href="/wiki/Yugoslavia" title="Yugoslavia">Yugoslavia</a> in the <a href="/wiki/20th_century" title="20th century">20th century</a>. After <a href="/wiki/NATO" title="NATO">NATO</a> bombed <a href="/wiki/Yugoslavia" title="Yugoslavia">Yugoslavia</a> in 1999, it was under the administration of the <a href="/wiki/United_Nations" title="United Nations">United Nations</a> (<a href="/w/index.php?title=UNMIK&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="UNMIK (not yet started)">UNMIK</a>). </p>

      <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kosovo&amp;veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Change section: Geography">change</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kosovo&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Change section: Geography">change source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
      <p>To the north and east of Kosovo is the <a href="/wiki/Serbia" title="Serbia">Republic of Serbia</a>. To the south of Kosovo is <a href="/wiki/North_Macedonia" title="North Macedonia">North Macedonia</a>. To the northwest is <a href="/wiki/Montenegro" title="Montenegro">Montenegro</a> and to the southwest is <a href="/wiki/Albania" title="Albania">Albania</a>. The <a href="/wiki/Capital_city" title="Capital city">capital</a> is <a href="/wiki/Pristina" title="Pristina">Pristina</a>. Prishtina is also Kosovo's largest city. About 1.8 million people live in Kosovo. </p>
